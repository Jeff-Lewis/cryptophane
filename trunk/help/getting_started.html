Start using Cryptophane

If you haven't already, take a look at the encryption primer.

Now that you know how assymetric cryptography works, you're ready to beign using it for yourself. Learn how to:

  • create a key pair
  • send your public key to a keyserver so other people can encrypt messages to you
  • encrypt and decrypt messages
  • sign messages and validate signed messages
  • check the fingerprint of a key on your keyring
  • sign a key on your keyring to show that you are sure who it came from
  • set the level of trust of a key on your keyring
